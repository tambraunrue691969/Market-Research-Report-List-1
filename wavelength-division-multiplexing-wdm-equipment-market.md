<p><p>The Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) equipment market comprises two main types: Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexing (CWDM) and Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing (DWDM). CWDM equipment allows multiple signals to be transmitted over a single fiber optic cable using fewer wavelengths and at wider spacing, making it cost-effective for short to medium distances. In contrast, DWDM equipment uses closely spaced wavelengths for higher capacity and long-distance transmission, delivering greater data rates and efficiency, crucial for modern telecommunications and data center applications.</p></p>
