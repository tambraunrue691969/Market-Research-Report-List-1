<p><p>The Silicon Carbide Ceramic Membrane market is primarily segmented into two types: Flat Ceramic Membrane and Tubular Ceramic Membrane. Conversely, Tubular Ceramic Membranes feature a cylindrical design, allowing for higher flow rates and resistance to fouling.
