<p><p>The lithography overcoats market comprises two main types: topcoats and overcoats. Topcoats are thin films applied to enhance photolithographic processes, providing protection to underlying layers and improving resolution. Overcoats, on the other hand, serve as additional protective layers that safeguard patterned features from contamination and environmental effects. Both types play a crucial role in semiconductor manufacturing, influencing the performance and reliability of electronic devices by enabling precise pattern fidelity and promoting better yields during production.</p></p>
