<p><p>The gasketed plate heat exchanger market is categorized into two main types: single circuit and multi circuit. Single circuit exchangers consist of one flow path for the heating and cooling media, making them straightforward and efficient for simpler thermal duties. In contrast, multi circuit exchangers feature multiple flow paths, allowing for greater flexibility and efficiency in handling various process requirements, including temperature gradients and fluid characteristics. This adaptability makes multi circuit designs suitable for complex applications across industries.</p></p>
