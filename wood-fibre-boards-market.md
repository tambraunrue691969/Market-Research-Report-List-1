Porous Wood Fibre Boards are lightweight and have a high permeability, making them suitable for applications requiring insulation and sound absorption. Hard Wood Fibre Boards, on the other hand, are denser and more durable, suitable for structural applications and furniture manufacturing. Both types cater to different needs in construction, interior design, and industrial applications, enhancing their market potential.</p></p>
