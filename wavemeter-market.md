<p><strong>The Wavemeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Michelson Interferometer-Based</li><li>Fizeau Interferometer-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wavemeter market features two primary types based on interferometer designs: Michelson interferometer-based and Fizeau interferometer-based wavemers. Michelson wavmeters utilize the interference patterns created by splitting and recombining light beams, offering high precision in measuring wavelengths, suitable for various scientific and industrial applications. In contrast, Fizeau wavemers employ a flat reference surface to create interference fringes, enabling quick wavelength measurements with a focus on simplicity and ease of use. Both types cater to diverse needs in optics and photonics industries.</p></p>
