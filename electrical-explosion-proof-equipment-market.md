<p><strong>The Electrical Explosion Proof Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flame-proof Type</li><li>Increased Safety Type</li><li>Intrinsic Safety Type</li><li>Positive-pressure Type</li><li>Oil-immersed Type</li><li>Sand Filled Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical explosion-proof equipment market comprises several types designed to prevent ignition in hazardous environments. Flame-proof type enclosures contain explosions and prevent external ignition. Increased safety type minimizes spark risks through enhanced design features. Intrinsic safety type limits energy in circuits to prevent ignition, while positive-pressure type maintains a protective atmosphere by using pressurized gas. Oil-immersed type is submerged in oil to cool and insulate, while sand-filled type uses sand to encase equipment, providing a physical barrier against ignitable elements.</p></p>
