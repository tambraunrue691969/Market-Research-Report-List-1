<p><strong>The Dried Whole Milk Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Regular Type</li><li>Instant Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dried whole milk market is categorized into two main types: Regular and Instant. Regular dried whole milk is produced by evaporating moisture from pasteurized milk, resulting in a powder that requires rehydration for use. Instant dried whole milk, on the other hand, undergoes a unique spray-drying process, allowing it to dissolve quickly in water without clumping. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, with regular often used in baking and cooking, while instant is favored for convenience in beverages and instant foods.</p></p>
