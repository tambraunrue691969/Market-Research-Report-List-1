<p><p>The capacitors market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Ceramic capacitors are widely used for their stability and reliability, whereas film capacitors provide low losses and high performance in audio applications. Electrolytic capacitors are favored for high-capacitance needs, often in power supplies. Supercapacitors excel in energy storage with rapid charge/discharge capabilities. Other types include tantalum and variable capacitors, serving niche markets.</p></p>
