<p><p>The Actinic Keratosis therapeutics market comprises three main types: topical medications, photodynamic therapy, and destructive treatments. Topical medications include creams and gels containing agents that promote skin cell turnover or reduce abnormal growths. Photodynamic therapy employs a photosensitizing agent activated by light to target and destroy affected cells. Destructive treatments involve physical removal techniques, such as cryotherapy, electrodessication, or curettage, effectively eliminating lesions. Each treatment option aims to reduce or eradicate pre-cancerous skin lesions, providing varying effectiveness and patient preferences.</p></p>
