<p><strong>The Intraoral X-ray Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bitewing X-rays</li><li>Periapical X-rays</li><li>Occlusal X-rays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intraoral X-ray imaging encompasses several types, including bitewing, periapical, and occlusal X-rays. Bitewing X-rays capture images of the upper and lower teeth simultaneously, aiding in cavity detection and assessing bite alignment. Periapical X-rays focus on individual teeth and surrounding bone structures, providing detailed views that are crucial for diagnosing various dental issues. Occlusal X-rays offer a wider perspective of the jaw and teeth alignment, useful for detecting abnormalities in larger areas of the mouth. Each type serves distinct diagnostic purposes in dental care.</p></p>
