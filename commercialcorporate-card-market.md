<p><strong>The Commercial/Corporate Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Loop</li><li>Closed Loop Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial/corporate card market is categorized into open-loop and closed-loop systems. Open-loop cards, typically associated with major networks like Visa or Mastercard, allow for a broad range of transactions across various merchants and locations. In contrast, closed-loop cards are issued by specific merchants or brands, restricting use to their owned channels or partner locations. This distinction affects transaction flexibility, acceptance, and rewards structures, catering to different business spending needs and travel management strategies.</p></p>
