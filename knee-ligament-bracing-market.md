<p><ul><li>Internal Support</li><li>External Support</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The knee ligament bracing market encompasses two main types: internal support and external support braces. Internal support braces are designed to provide stability and support from within the joint, often using materials that mimic the natural ligaments. In contrast, external support braces focus on stabilizing the knee from the outside, utilizing straps and rigid frames to limit movement and protect the knee during rehabilitation or physical activities. Both types cater to various injury levels, activity needs, and recovery stages.</p></p>
