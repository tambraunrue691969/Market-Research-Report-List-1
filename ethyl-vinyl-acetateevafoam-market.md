<p><p>The Ethyl Vinyl Acetate (EVA) foam market can be segmented into three main production methods: extrusion molding, injection foaming molding, and compression molding. Extrusion molding involves forming foam by forcing melted EVA through a die, creating continuous sheets or shapes. Injection foaming molding combines EVA with a gas and injects it into a mold, allowing for complex shapes with precise dimensions. Compression molding uses heat and pressure to shape EVA foam in a mold, providing high-density products suited for various applications.</p></p>
