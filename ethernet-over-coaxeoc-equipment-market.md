<p><p>Ethernet over Coax (EoC) equipment is categorized into two main types: Passive EoC and Active EoC. </p><p>Passive EoC devices utilize existing coaxial cable infrastructure without requiring additional power or signal amplification, making them cost-effective for extending Ethernet services. In contrast, Active EoC solutions incorporate powered devices that boost signal strength and manage data traffic over coaxial lines, enabling longer distances and higher performance. Both types cater to different deployment needs in various environments, enhancing network connectivity and efficiency.</p></p>
