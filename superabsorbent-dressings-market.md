<p><p>The superabsorbent dressings market is categorized into two main types: non-adhering dressings and self-adhering dressings. Non-adhering dressings are designed to prevent sticking to the wound, minimizing pain during removal and allowing for easier dressing changes. In contrast, self-adhering dressings feature an integrated adhesive that allows them to securely attach to the skin without additional tape, providing stability and convenience. Both types aim to manage exudate effectively, promoting a conducive environment for wound healing while enhancing patient comfort.</p></p>
