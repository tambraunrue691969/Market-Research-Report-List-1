<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membrane</li><li>High Flux Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flat type dialyzer market comprises two primary segments: low flux and high flux membranes. Low flux membranes are designed for patients with stable renal function, providing slower filtration but preserving essential solutes, which is ideal for certain clinical scenarios. In contrast, high flux membranes allow for greater clearance of larger molecules and toxins, enhancing treatment efficacy for patients with more advanced kidney disease. Both types cater to varying patient needs and therapeutic objectives in dialysis.</p></p>
