<p><p>The Automotive Engine Emission Control System market comprises several key components designed to reduce harmful emissions. The oxygen sensor measures air-fuel mixture efficiency, while the EGR valve recirculates exhaust gases to lower nitrogen oxide emissions. The catalytic converter facilitates chemical reactions to convert harmful pollutants into less harmful substances. The air pump helps ensure complete combustion by supplying additional air, and the PCV valve recycles vacuum gases, reducing emissions. Lastly, the charcoal canister traps fuel vapors, preventing them from escaping into the atmosphere.</p></p>
