<p><p>The Bilirubin Colorimeter Assay Service Market is segmented into two main types: Conjugated Bilirubin (Direct Bilirubin) and Unconjugated Bilirubin (Indirect Bilirubin). Conjugated Bilirubin refers to bilirubin that has undergone conjugation, making it water-soluble and easily excretable, typically indicating liver function and bile duct health. Unconjugated Bilirubin, on the other hand, is not yet processed by the liver, implying potential issues like hemolysis or liver dysfunction. Both types are crucial for diagnosing and monitoring liver diseases and hemolytic conditions.</p></p>
