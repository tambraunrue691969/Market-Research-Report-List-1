<p><strong>The Passenger Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger vehicle wheel speed sensor market primarily features two types: Hall effect sensors and magneto-electric sensors. Hall effect sensors use a semiconductor element to detect the presence of a magnetic field, providing accurate wheel speed readings. In contrast, magneto-electric sensors generate an electric signal in response to the rotation of a magnetic rotor, translating physical speed into electrical output. Both types are essential for vehicle stability control, anti-lock braking systems, and overall vehicle safety, playing vital roles in modern automotive technology.</p></p>
