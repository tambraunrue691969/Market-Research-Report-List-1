<p><p>The Vehicle Lighting Fixtures Market includes Halogen, HID, and LED lighting types. Halogen lighting is cost-effective and widely used, offering bright, warm light but with higher energy consumption. HID (High-Intensity Discharge) lighting provides intense, white light with better efficiency than halogen but at a higher cost. LED (Light Emitting Diode) lighting is the most advanced, offering superior energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and compact design, though it is more expensive initially. Each type caters to different consumer needs, balancing performance, cost, and durability.</p></p>
