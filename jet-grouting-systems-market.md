<p><strong>The Jet-Grouting Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>The Single-Phase System</li><li>The Double-Phase System</li><li>The Three-Phase System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jet-grouting systems market encompasses three primary types based on the fluid delivery process: </p><p>1. **Single-Phase System** uses one fluid, typically water or grout, injected at high pressure to mix with soil, creating a solid mass.  </p><p>2. **Double-Phase System** combines a liquid and air, enhancing the mixing and stabilizing process, allowing for greater control over the material properties.  </p><p>3. **Three-Phase System** integrates liquid, air, and a solid material, increasing the strength and bind properties, suitable for challenging soil conditions. Each system caters to unique geotechnical applications.</p></p>
