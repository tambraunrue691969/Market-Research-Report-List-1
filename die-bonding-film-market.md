<p><p>The Die Bonding Film market is categorized into Non-Conductive and Conductive types based on electrical properties. Non-Conductive Die Bonding Films are used in applications requiring electrical insulation, such as in semiconductor packaging where electrical isolation between components is critical. Conductive Die Bonding Films, on the other hand, provide electrical connectivity, making them ideal for applications like power devices or LEDs where efficient heat dissipation and electrical conduction are essential. Both types cater to diverse industry needs in advanced electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
