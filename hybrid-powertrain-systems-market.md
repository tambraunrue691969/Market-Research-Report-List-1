<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Hybrid Powertrain Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Hybrid</li><li>Series Hybrid</li><li>Series-parallel Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid powertrain systems combine internal combustion engines (ICE) with electric motors to improve efficiency and reduce emissions. **Parallel Hybrid** systems allow both the ICE and electric motor to power the vehicle simultaneously or independently, offering flexibility and performance. **Series Hybrid** systems use the ICE solely to generate electricity for the electric motor, which drives the wheels, ideal for stop-and-go driving. **Series-Parallel Hybrid** systems integrate both approaches, enabling the vehicle to switch between series and parallel modes for optimal efficiency and power delivery across various driving conditions.</p></p>
