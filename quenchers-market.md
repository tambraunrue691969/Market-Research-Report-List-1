<p><p>The Quenchers market includes Dark Quenchers and Fluorescent Quenchers, both used in molecular biology and diagnostics. Dark Quenchers absorb light without emitting fluorescence, reducing background noise in assays like qPCR and FRET. Fluorescent Quenchers, on the other hand, absorb energy from a donor fluorophore and re-emit it at a different wavelength, enabling multiplex detection. Both types enhance assay sensitivity and specificity, catering to applications in genomics, proteomics, and drug discovery, driving demand in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
