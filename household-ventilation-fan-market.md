<p><p>The household ventilation fan market consists of various types designed to enhance indoor air circulation and comfort. Ceiling fans are mounted on ceilings and are effective for large spaces, providing even airflow. Wall-mounted fans are designed for smaller areas, offering directed ventilation while saving floor space. Window-mounted fans are installed in windows to draw in fresh air or exhaust stale air, promoting cross-ventilation in homes. Each type serves specific needs, contributing significantly to overall household air quality and comfort.</p></p>
