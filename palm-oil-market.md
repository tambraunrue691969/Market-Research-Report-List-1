<p><strong>The Palm Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crude Palm Oil</li><li>Palm Olein</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The palm oil market comprises two primary types: Crude Palm Oil (CPO) and Palm Olein. CPO is the unrefined oil extracted directly from palm fruit and serves as the base product for various derivatives. In contrast, Palm Olein is the liquid fraction derived from CPO after fractionation, making it suitable for cooking and food products due to its stability and lower saturated fat content. Both types are crucial in the global edible oil industry, catering to diverse consumer preferences and industrial applications.</p></p>
