<p><p>The concrete construction materials market is primarily segmented into three types: Ready-Mix Concrete, On-Site Mixed Concrete, and Others. Ready-Mix Concrete is pre-mixed at a batching plant and delivered to construction sites, ensuring consistent quality and efficiency. On-Site Mixed Concrete is produced directly at the construction location, allowing for flexibility in mix designs and reducing transportation costs. The "Others" category encompasses specialized concrete types, such as precast concrete and high-performance concrete, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
