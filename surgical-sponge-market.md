<p><p>The surgical sponge market comprises three main types: cotton gauze sponges, nonwoven sponges, and x-ray detectable sponges. Cotton gauze sponges are absorbent, versatile, and commonly used for wound dressing and surgical procedures. Nonwoven sponges offer enhanced fluid management and reduced linting, making them suitable for critical applications. X-ray detectable sponges contain radiopaque materials that ensure they can be identified post-surgery, preventing retention inside patients. Each type serves distinct purposes in surgical settings, prioritizing safety and efficacy.</p></p>
