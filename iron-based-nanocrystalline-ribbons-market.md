<p><ul><li>Vertical magnetic field annealing Type</li><li>Ordinary annealing</li><li>Transverse magnetic field annealing Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Iron-based nanocrystalline ribbons are produced using different annealing techniques that influence their magnetic properties. The vertical magnetic field annealing type enhances magnetic anisotropy by aligning grains in a vertical field, while ordinary annealing focuses on heat treatment without external magnetic influences, leading to adequate performance but less customization. Transverse magnetic field annealing type utilizes a horizontal magnetic field to optimize magnetic characteristics, promoting uniformity in material properties. Each method caters to specific applications in electronics and energy sectors.</p></p>
