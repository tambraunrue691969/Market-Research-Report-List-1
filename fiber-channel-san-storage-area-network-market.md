<p><p>Fiber Channel SAN (Storage Area Network) is a high-speed network designed for connecting data storage devices. The market can be divided into three primary types: Copper, Single-Mode Optical Fiber, and Multi-Mode Optical Fiber. Copper cables are cost-effective for shorter distances, while Single-Mode Optical Fiber supports long-range connections with higher bandwidth. Multi-Mode Optical Fiber provides a balance between performance and distance, making it suitable for mid-range environments. Each type serves distinct needs based on performance and distance requirements in storage connectivity.</p></p>
