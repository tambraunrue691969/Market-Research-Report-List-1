<p><p>The Automated Endoscope Reprocessors (AER) market is categorized into two main types: single chamber and multi-chamber systems. Single chamber AERs perform cleaning and disinfection in one integrated unit, offering convenience and space efficiency, making them suitable for smaller facilities. In contrast, multi-chamber AERs possess separate compartments for each cleaning stage, which enhances the thoroughness of the disinfection process and accommodates higher volumes of endoscope processing. Both types aim to improve patient safety and streamline workflow in healthcare settings.</p></p>
