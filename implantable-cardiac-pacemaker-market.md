<p><p>The implantable cardiac pacemaker market encompasses devices designed to regulate heart rhythms. Pacemakers provide electrical stimulation to manage bradycardia, ensuring a regular heartbeat. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks to correct life-threatening arrhythmias. Biventricular ICDs, or BI-V ICDs, further enhance heart function by coordinating contractions in patients with heart failure via dual lead systems. Each type targets specific cardiac conditions, contributing to improved patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
