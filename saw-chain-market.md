<p><strong>The Saw Chain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Chisel</li><li>Semi Chisel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The saw chain market is primarily divided into two types: Full Chisel and Semi Chisel. Full Chisel chains feature square-shaped cutter teeth, providing faster cutting speed and reduced friction, making them ideal for professionals and hardwood applications. However, they dull more quickly. In contrast, Semi Chisel chains have rounded cutter teeth, offering better durability and greater resistance to dulling, making them suitable for softer woods and dirty conditions. Each type caters to specific user needs and operational environments.</p></p>
