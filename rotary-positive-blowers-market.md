<p><p>Rotary positive blowers are categorized into several types based on their design and operation. Straight bi-lobe blowers feature two lobes, efficiently moving air with minimal pulsation. Straight tri-lobe blowers utilize three lobes for increased airflow and quieter operation. Twisted tri-lobe blowers have a twisted design for more efficient air movement and reduced vibration. Helical screw blowers employ a screw mechanism for continuous airflow, making them suitable for various applications. Other blowers include specialized designs for niche requirements, enhancing their versatility in industries.</p></p>
