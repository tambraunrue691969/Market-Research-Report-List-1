<p><p>The adhesives for LED packaging market consists of three primary types: liquid, solid, and paste. Liquid adhesives are often used for their ease of application and ability to fill gaps, providing a strong bond while accommodating thermal expansion. Solid adhesives, typically in the form of films or sheets, offer robust mechanical strength and are ideal for high-temperature applications. Paste adhesives, known for their high viscosity, are used for precise applications where control during application is essential, ensuring optimal performance and reliability in LED assemblies.</p></p>
