<p><p>The Metal Injection Molding (MIM) Furnace market encompasses two primary types: batch furnaces and continuous furnaces. Batch furnaces process smaller quantities of metal injection molded parts in discrete loads, offering flexibility and suitability for varied production demands. In contrast, continuous furnaces enable a steady flow of materials, optimizing efficiency and productivity for large-scale operations. Each type caters to different manufacturing needs, from prototyping and low-volume production to high-output manufacturing, influencing their application in diverse industries.</p></p>
