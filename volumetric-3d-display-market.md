<p><strong>The Volumetric 3D Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Swept-Volume Display</li><li>Solid-Volume Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The volumetric 3D display market is primarily divided into two types: swept-volume displays and solid-volume displays. Swept-volume displays create 3D visuals by projecting images onto a rotating surface, allowing for dynamic viewing angles and motion. In contrast, solid-volume displays generate images within a defined space, utilizing techniques like point-cloud rendering or volumetric pixels, offering a more immersive experience. Both types serve applications in entertainment, medical imaging, and simulation, enhancing user interaction with three-dimensional content.</p></p>
