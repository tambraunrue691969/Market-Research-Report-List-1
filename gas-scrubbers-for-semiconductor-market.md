<p><p>Gas scrubbers in the semiconductor market are essential for removing harmful pollutants and maintaining clean environments. Burn scrubbers utilize high temperatures to oxidize gaseous waste, while plasma scrubbers employ ionized gases to break down contaminants. Heat wet scrubbers use heated water to absorb and neutralize gases, whereas dry scrubbers utilize solid adsorbents to capture particulates and chemicals. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring environmental compliance and enhancing air quality within semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
