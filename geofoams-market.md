<p><strong>The Geofoams Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPS Geofoams</li><li>XPS Geofoams</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geofoams market primarily consists of two types: EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) geofoams and XPS (Extruded Polystyrene) geofoams. EPS geofoams are lightweight, cost-effective, and offer good insulation properties, making them ideal for a variety of civil engineering applications, including lightweight fill and slope stabilization. In contrast, XPS geofoams provide superior compressive strength and moisture resistance, making them suitable for applications requiring higher durability, such as under roadways and other load-bearing structures, enhancing overall performance in various projects.</p></p>
