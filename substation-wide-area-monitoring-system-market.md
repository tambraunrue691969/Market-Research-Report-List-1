<p><p>The Substation Wide Area Monitoring System market consists of various components. Phasor Measurement Units (PMUs) gather real-time data on electrical parameters, enabling precise monitoring of grid stability. Phasor Data Concentrators (PDCs) aggregate data from multiple PMUs for analysis and reporting. Communication networks facilitate the transmission of this data, ensuring timely and reliable information flow. Additionally, PDC solutions encompass software and hardware that optimize data processing and enhance grid operations, providing utilities with critical insights for efficient management and decision-making.</p></p>
