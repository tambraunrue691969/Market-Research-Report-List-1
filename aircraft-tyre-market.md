<p><p>The aircraft tire market is primarily categorized into two main types: main tires and front tires. Main tires, also known as main landing gear tires, are designed to support the bulk of the aircraft's weight during takeoff and landing, providing stability and durability under heavy loads. Front tires, or nose gear tires, are smaller and primarily assist with steering and balance during ground operations. Both tire types are engineered for high performance, ensuring safety and efficiency in various flight conditions.</p></p>
