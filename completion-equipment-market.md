<p><ul><li>Casings</li><li>Cementing Materials & Equipment</li><li>Perforating Guns</li><li>Gravel Packing</li><li>Wellheads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Completion Equipment Market encompasses various components essential for finalizing oil and gas wells. Casings provide structural integrity and protect wellbore integrity. Cementing materials and equipment ensure proper sealing of the annulus between casings and the formation, preventing leaks. Perforating guns create channels through the casing and cement, allowing hydrocarbons to flow. Gravel packing involves installing gravel to prevent sand production. Wellheads serve as the interface between the wellbore and surface facilities, maintaining pressure control and facilitating extraction operations.</p></p>
