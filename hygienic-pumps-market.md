<p><p>The hygienic pumps market consists of centrifugal and positive displacement types, along with other specialized pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotating impellers to move fluids, making them ideal for high flow rates in sanitary applications. Positive displacement pumps, on the other hand, move fluid by trapping a fixed amount and forcing it through the discharge, ensuring accurate flow even at varying pressure conditions. Other market segments may include peristaltic and diaphragm pumps, designed for specific hygienic requirements in food, pharmaceutical, and biotechnology industries.</p></p>
