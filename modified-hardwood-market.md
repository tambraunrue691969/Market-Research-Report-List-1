<p><p>Modified hardwood market types include thermal treatment, acetylation, and other treatments, each enhancing wood's durability and stability. Thermal treatment involves heating wood to increase resistance to moisture and insects, resulting in a darker appearance. Acetylation uses acetic anhydride to replace hydroxyl groups in wood, improving water resistance and dimensional stability. Other treatments encompass a range of methods such as chemical infusions and pressure treatments, enhancing performance and aesthetic qualities, making them suitable for various applications in construction and furniture.</p></p>
