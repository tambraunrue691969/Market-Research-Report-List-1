<p><p>The MDF crown moulding market features three primary types: fire-retardant MDF, moisture-resistant MDF, and general MDF. Fire-retardant MDF is designed to withstand high temperatures and reduce flame spread, making it ideal for safety-conscious applications. Moisture-resistant MDF offers durability in humid environments, preventing warping and deterioration, suitable for kitchens and bathrooms. General MDF serves as a versatile and cost-effective option, widely used in various interior applications due to its smooth finish and ease of machining, appealing to general construction and design needs.</p></p>
