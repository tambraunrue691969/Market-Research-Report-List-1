<p><p>The Mobile Boat Hoists market comprises two main types: self-propelled boat hoists and towed boat hoists. Self-propelled boat hoists are equipped with their own motors, allowing them to maneuver independently to transport boats efficiently. They offer greater flexibility and speed, making them ideal for busy marinas. In contrast, towed boat hoists are designed to be towed by another vehicle, providing a cost-effective solution for lifting and transporting boats. Both types cater to different operational needs and user preferences in boat handling.</p></p>
