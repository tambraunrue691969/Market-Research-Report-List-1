<p><ul><li>Mechanical Lidar</li><li>Solid State Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Lidar System market comprises Mechanical Lidar and Solid-State Lidar. Mechanical Lidar uses rotating components to emit laser beams and capture 3D environmental data, offering high resolution but with bulkier designs and higher costs. Solid-State Lidar, on the other hand, relies on stationary components, providing compact, durable, and cost-effective solutions, though with slightly lower resolution. Both types are integral to autonomous vehicles, enhancing perception and safety, but Solid-State Lidar is gaining traction due to its scalability and reliability in automotive applications.</p></p>
