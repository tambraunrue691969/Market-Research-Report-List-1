<p><p>The industrial carbohydrase market comprises three main types: amylase, glucanase, and cellulase. Amylase is primarily used for starch hydrolysis in food and beverage industries, enhancing sugar production. Glucanase breaks down glucan in various substrates, playing a crucial role in biofuel production and animal feed. Cellulase targets cellulose, facilitating the conversion of plant material into fermentable sugars, vital for paper, textiles, and biofuel industries. Together, these enzymes drive innovation and efficiency across multiple sectors, promoting sustainable practices.</p></p>
