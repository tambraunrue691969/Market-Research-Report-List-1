<p><strong>The Marine Cylinder Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-BN (70-100 BN)</li><li>Low-BN (15-60 BN)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine cylinder oils are specialized lubricants used in marine engines to protect against wear, corrosion, and deposits. They are categorized based on their Base Number (BN), which indicates their ability to neutralize acidic byproducts from fuel combustion. **High-BN (70-100 BN)** oils are designed for engines burning high-sulfur fuels, offering superior acid neutralization. **Low-BN (15-60 BN)** oils are suited for low-sulfur fuels, providing efficient lubrication with less alkalinity. Both types ensure optimal engine performance and longevity, tailored to specific fuel types and operational requirements.</p></p>
