<p><p>The retread robots market includes several types tailored for specific tasks. Articulated robots feature multiple rotational joints, offering flexibility for complex movements. Cartesian robots operate on three linear axes, ideal for precise applications. Cylindrical robots utilize a cylindrical coordinate system, combining linear and rotational abilities for versatile tasks. Polar robots have a rotating base and extendable arm, suitable for wide-reaching applications. SCARA robots are designed for high-speed tasks with vertical movement, while Delta robots are optimized for high-speed pick-and-place operations in confined spaces.</p></p>
