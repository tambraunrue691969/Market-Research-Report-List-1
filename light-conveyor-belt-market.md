<p><p>The Light Conveyor Belt market can be segmented into two primary types based on manufacturing processes: Coating Process and Calendering Process. The Coating Process involves applying a layer of material onto a substrate, enhancing properties like durability and friction. Conversely, the Calendering Process involves passing materials through rollers to achieve specific thickness and texture, resulting in consistent and uniform belts. Both processes aim to optimize performance and versatility of light conveyor belts in various industrial applications.</p></p>
