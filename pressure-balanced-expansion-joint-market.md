<p><p>The Pressure Balanced Expansion Joint market consists of two main types: Inline and Elbow Type. Inline expansion joints are designed to accommodate axial movements in straight pipe runs, effectively absorbing thermal expansion and contraction. Elbow Type joints, on the other hand, are utilized in systems with directional changes, allowing for angular adjustments while maintaining pressure. Both types are essential in various industrial applications, as they enhance system flexibility, reduce stress on piping, and prevent leaks in high-pressure environments.</p></p>
