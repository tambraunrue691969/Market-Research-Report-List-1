<p><p>Button lithium cells are categorized into two main types: primary and secondary batteries. Primary batteries, also known as non-rechargeable batteries, provide a one-time use energy source, ideal for low-drain devices like watches and hearing aids. In contrast, secondary batteries, or rechargeable batteries, can be reused multiple times, making them suitable for applications that require frequent power cycling, such as cameras and medical equipment. Both types are pivotal in enhancing the performance and efficiency of compact electronic devices.</p></p>
