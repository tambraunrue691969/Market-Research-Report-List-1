<p><p>Remote Patient Monitoring Systems encompass various types of devices designed to track patients' health metrics from a distance. Vital signs monitors assess essential indicators like temperature and oxygen levels. Glucose monitors track blood sugar levels, vital for diabetes management. Blood pressure monitors measure arterial pressure, aiding in cardiovascular health. Heart rate monitors capture pulse rates, reflecting cardiac activity. Respiratory monitors evaluate breathing patterns, crucial for lung health. "Others" may include devices for tracking weight, medication adherence, or additional health parameters.</p></p>
