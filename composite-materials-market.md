<p><p>The composite materials market is segmented into three main types: Polymer Matrix Composites (PMCs), Metal Matrix Composites (MMCs), and Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMCs). PMCs are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, making them ideal for automotive and aerospace applications. MMCs offer enhanced strength and thermal resistance, suitable for high-performance mechanical applications. CMCs are known for their ability to withstand high temperatures and are primarily used in aerospace and industrial sectors. Together, these composites provide tailored properties for diverse applications.</p></p>
