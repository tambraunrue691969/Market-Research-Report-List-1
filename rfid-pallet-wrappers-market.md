<p><strong>The RFID Pallet Wrappers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultra-High Frequency</li><li>High Frequency and Near Field Communication</li><li>Low Frequency</li><li>Dual Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID pallet wrappers are categorized by frequency types, which influence their performance and applications. Ultra-High Frequency (UHF) operates at 300 MHz to 3 GHz, offering longer reading ranges suitable for supply chain logistics. High Frequency (HF) functions at 13.56 MHz, ideal for shorter distances and environments with dense materials. Near Field Communication (NFC), a subset of HF, facilitates communication over very short ranges, often for consumer interactions. Low Frequency (LF) operates from 30 kHz to 300 kHz, providing reliable performance in challenging conditions. Dual Frequency combines advantages of multiple frequencies, enhancing flexibility and efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
