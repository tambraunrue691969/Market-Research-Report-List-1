<p><p>The Laser Particle Size Instrument market is categorized into two primary types: dry test and wet test. Dry test methods involve dispersing particles in a dry state, allowing for quick analysis of solid materials, commonly used in industries like pharmaceuticals and minerals. Wet test methods, on the other hand, use a liquid medium to disperse particles, which is ideal for analyzing powders that are difficult to test dry, such as in chemicals and food production. Each method offers distinct advantages depending on the application.</p></p>
