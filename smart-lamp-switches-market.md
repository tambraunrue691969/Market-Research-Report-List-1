<p><p>The Smart Lamp Switches market features two primary types: Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. Wi-Fi smart switches connect to local networks, allowing remote control via smartphones or voice assistants over the internet. They often offer broader range and integration with smart home ecosystems. Conversely, Bluetooth smart switches operate over shorter distances, enabling direct device pairing without a Wi-Fi connection. They are typically easier to install and more cost-effective but may lack extensive functionality. Both types enhance home automation and user convenience.</p></p>
