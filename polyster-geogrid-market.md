<p><p>The polyester geogrid market is divided into two primary types: Regular-Tow Geogrid and Large-Tow Geogrid. Regular-Tow Geogrids feature smaller, closely-packed fibers, providing excellent tensile strength and stability for applications like road construction and soil reinforcement. In contrast, Large-Tow Geogrids utilize thicker fibers, enhancing durability and load distribution, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications such as embankments and retaining structures. Each type addresses specific engineering needs, contributing to improved performance and longevity in geotechnical projects.</p></p>
