<p><p>The bio-organic fertilizer market comprises two main types: organic residue fertilizers and microorganisms (biofertilizers). Organic residue fertilizers are derived from plant and animal waste, enriching soil with nutrients and enhancing its structure. They improve soil fertility and promote sustainable agricultural practices. On the other hand, microorganisms or biofertilizers are living entities, such as bacteria and fungi, that stimulate plant growth by enhancing nutrient availability and promoting root development. Together, they contribute to healthier crops and environmentally friendly farming methods.</p></p>
